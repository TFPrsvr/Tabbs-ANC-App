Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Privacy First: ANC Audio Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. We minimize data collection and prioritize local processing whenever possible.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Audio Processing Data

  • Local Processing: Audio files are processed locally on your device whenever possible
  • Temporary Processing: For advanced AI features, audio may be temporarily processed on secure servers and immediately deleted
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your audio content on our servers

1.2 Usage Analytics

  • Performance metrics to improve app functionality
  • Feature usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Error reports for debugging purposes
  • Device and browser information for compatibility

1.3 Account Information

  • Email address for account creation and support
  • Profile preferences and settings
  • Subscription and billing information (processed by secure third parties)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Audio Processing: To provide advanced AI-powered audio processing features
  • Service Improvement: To enhance app performance and user experience
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Communication: To send important updates about your account or the service

3. Data Security

  • Military-Grade Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use enterprise-grade security measures and regular security audits
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data
  • Automatic Deletion: Temporary processing data is automatically deleted within 24 hours

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate the service (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and non-essential data collection

6. International Users

ANC Audio Pro complies with international privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws. Users outside the United States have the same privacy rights and protections.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@ancaudiopro.com
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy requests within 30 days
